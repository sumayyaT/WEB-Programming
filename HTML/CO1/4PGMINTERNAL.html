COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING VADAKARA

College of engineering vadakara is formly known ad co operative institute and technology,vadakara.Is the first engineering college under co operative academy of professional education(CAPE)established by government of kerala in the year 1999.CEV located kurunthodi in maniyur village 13km away from vadakara railway station,vadakara is 14 km away from the campus

COLLEGE FACILITY

For transportation of students and staff,the college has self sufficient transportation facility managed by the college bus committee,under the ownership.other facilities are college canteen,cafetheeria,hostels,libraries...etc.
